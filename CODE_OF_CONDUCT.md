# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- respectful communication
- constructive feedback
- empathy and patience
- focusing on project goals and technical quality

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private information
- abusive language or intimidation
- disruptive trolling and bad-faith arguments

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Please report unacceptable behavior via project issue tracker:
https://github.com/IsKenKenYa/1Panel-Client/issues
